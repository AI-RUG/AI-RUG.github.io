Hi! We're the Artificial Intelligence (AI) promoteam at the university of Groningen. We organise the AI-related promotional and informational activities at the University of Groningen and at high schools throughout the Netherlands.

We will be using this website to host our practical assignments and workshops. Feel free to take a look around! Assignments

This is where you can find the assignments we have designed. They are mainly meant to be used during specific activities, and are accompanied by presentations given by our team. Feel free to look at them, but they may not make complete sense without context.

Introduction to Programming

This assignment is used during the Student for a Day of Artificial Intelligence, to provide a small introduction to programming. The excercises and explanations are designed to be doable yet slightly challening for both complete beginners and people who have a little bit of experience with programming.
